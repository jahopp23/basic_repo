Blobwall ~

"BlobWall, a fun slime morphing hole in the wall game made for the Ludum Dare 35, has you stretching yourself out into various shapes to fill the spaces in walls!" -Alpha Beta Gamer

Controls: move with arrow keys hold space as you move to fill out a shape, release space to go back to being a blob.

Fail to match the shape completely and some of the edge will fall away.
